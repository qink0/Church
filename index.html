About us

About us

Primm Tabernacle African Methodist Episcopal Church was organized as a mission church on November 5, 1961, by Pastor Edward N. Smith Jr. Primm was incorporated on January 5, 1966. Pastor Smith's goal was that church would serve the residents of the Holly Park housing projects and the Beacon Hill Area of Seattle.

"One Spirit... Lifting Him Up!"

Love

At our intimate church, love is at the core of everything we do. We believe that love is the foundation of our faith and strive to share God's love with all who come into our midst.

Shelter

In our church, we aim to be a shelter for those seeking solace, guidance, and spiritual nourishment. We understand that life can be challenging, and we believe that everyone deserves a safe haven where they can find comfort and refuge.

Come Back

If you have drifted away from your faith or are searching for a spiritual home, we invite you to come back and find your place within our church community. We understand that life's challenges can sometimes lead us astray, but we believe that no matter how far you may have wandered, there is always a way back.

A Place To Talk

In addition to being a house of worship, our church is also a place to talk, share, and grow together. We recognize the importance of community and the power of conversation in nurturing our faith and supporting one another. Our church provides various opportunities for fellowships.
